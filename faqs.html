Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Questions We Hear Often

Click to expand FAQ

Q. What type of straw do you sell and use?

The standard product is "standard" Slash and "Deluxe" Long leaf straw. All bales are hand raked daily and loaded into tractor trailers. We install the pine straw, tuck in row and then blow and clean the area before leaving.

Q. What is the price of pine straw?

Prices vary depending on the type of pine straw and the delivery location. Please contact us for a quote. We will be happy to assist you.

Q. Do you sell to the public?

We tend to work with clients who manage golf courses, hospitals, gated type communities and contractors who offer landscaping services. We also sell to the public.

Q. When placing an order, what is the waiting time to receive a load?

Orders placed are usually delivered within 1-2 business days depending on the schedule from the previous week.

Q. Do you deliver outside the state of Georgia?

Our main focus is on serving our Georgia community. The vast majority of our clients are Georgia based. That being said, we would love to talk to you about arrangements for service outside of this area.

Q. Are you insured?

We are fully insured and a certificate of insurance is provided upon request.
