WE STRIVE TO EXCEED YOUR EXPECTATIONS

At Nunez Pine Straw, Inc. we strive to meet your expectations. Our goal is to provide you with courteous service and a quality product. We are a family owned & operated company. Our company is located in southeast Georgia, where all the pine straw is harvested daily.


Our bales of pine straw are hand-raked and each bale is machine compressed and tied by hand with string. This method has resulted in a far more superior product. Since we are involved with the entire production cycle, from the initial harvesting all the way to the delivery location, we guarantee the quality of our product.
We would like to take this opportunity to invite you to try our product and to discover the advantages that pine straw offers for all your mulch needs. If we can answer any questions, please let us know.
